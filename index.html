about me

I am a Professional UI/UX Designer and Web developer. I also know a few programming languages which is where I started: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Lua and Python (Mostly for SQL purposes). I have a lot of experience with 3D modelling as well, where I have used software such as Blender & Adobe Substance Painter and mostly used this skill for Garry's Mod as well as mods for other games such as Ready or Not and Ground Branch. I grew up playing video games and have been fascinated with creating them ever since.

My journey began playing the sandbox game Garry's Mod. On my long road that you can read at My Experiences you'll follow along and learn about how I came to be, how I started, why I started and what I'm doing now.

      Malmö, Sweden

My grades were very messed up and I only had about a term (5 months) to turn a negative into a positive, and thats what I did.

      I took school very seriously and I got graded with everything being above average with a couple of Bs and As, whereas one subject got a grade with an unfortunate F. That being math.
